Who we are

Have you ever zoned out and watched the food channels for hours on end, drooling over the mouthwatering goodness? How often have you actually made these dishes post-couch coma? Well have I got the food-delivery service you've been waiting for!

Introducing EasyMeals - the easiest and most convenient way to make those dishes you dreamed of! With the click of a button, select the featured meal of your choice, the servings you're looking for and the food will be delivered to your door ready to be transformed into a tantalizing meal!

Our company's pure focus is making the food you've been craving from our favourite network and online food channels accessible and attainable. No fussy prep work to do, no shopping lists to make only to forget at home, just good food made into easy meals you've already seen before.

What we do

It's simple. We spend a lot of our time watching food videos just like you do. We take the ones that we actually want to make and compile, measure and prep everything you need to make this at home. All you need is a couple minutes to throw it together based on the instructions provided.

Along with the ingredients and recipe, we'll also send you media like website links and videos to guide you along. You get a great meal and some entertainment - what more could you want?!
